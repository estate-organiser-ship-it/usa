Hi, I'm Will

Founder of The Estate Organizer

I'm not a lawyer. I'm not a financial planner. I'm someone who watched two families, mine and my partner's, try to sort out an estate, and realized how impossible it is when nothing's written down.

Bank accounts no one knew about. Old retirement accounts from past jobs. Insurance policies buried in a drawer. Passwords lost forever. Wishes that were never shared. And on top of it all, the grief of losing someone.

I started looking for a better way and ran straight into a wall. Estate planning is a black box. Nobody gives you straight answers. Every conversation feels like a guessing game where the only people who really understand it charge by the hour.

So I built what I wished my family had: one document that holds what an executor needs. Plain language. No jargon. No legal degree required. Just the practical handover your family will be desperate for one day, done quietly, on your own time, before it's needed.

The Estate Organizer is for people like me. People who love their families and don't want them left guessing.
